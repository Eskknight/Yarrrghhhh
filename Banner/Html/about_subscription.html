About Subscription

What do I get with a SketchBook subscription?

Access advanced tools in SketchBook. Get every update and new feature on all your devices. Create unlimited scenes in SketchBook Motion."

How do I access my subscription on other devices?

Log in using the same Autodesk username and password you used when purchasing your subscription.

When will my subscription be renewed?

Subscriptions are automatically renewed, unless auto-renew is turned off at least 24-hours before the end of the current subscription period.

When will I be charged?

Your account will be charged for renewal within 24-hours prior to the end of the current subscription period and will state the cost of the renewal.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Once you've made a purchase, manage your subscription, including auto-renewal, through your Account Settings.

Do I lose any of my work when I unsubscribe?

No. You will still have access to all your files and the basic free tools. However, please be aware that deleting the app will delete all your files.
